Welcome to RPS!

The repeated rock-scissor-paper (RPS) game is a game of strategy, and you'll be at an advantage if you can predict your opponent's next move, while keeping yourself unpredictable. A 2 year-old repeatedly playing scissors is an very predictable opponent.

Humans are not very good at being random. We often display tendencies, like in RPS, we might unintentionally avoid repeating a 'scissors' right after we played 'scissors' and lost. Recognizing and exploiting such tendencies allows a player to gain an advantage over an opponent.
