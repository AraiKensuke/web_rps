We ask that you play 2 10-game rounds of repeated RPS lasting against simple bots that generate RPS moves using simple rules that depend on your and/or the bot's last moves. If you can figure out the bot's rules, you should be able to predict the bot's next move.

You can go straight to the 18 rounds, or you can first practice playing RPS against the computer in short, 10 game rounds, and then go on to the 2 rounds when you're ready.
